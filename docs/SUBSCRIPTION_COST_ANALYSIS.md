# AI Image Generation - Subscription Cost Analysis

**Date**: April 18, 2026  
**Context**: Paragu-AI Builder - Need 33 images for 3 website tenants

---

## 💰 Subscription Options Comparison

### **Option 1: Continue with Gemini Free Plan**

**Cost**: $0/month

**Limits**:
- 15-30 image generations per day (varies)
- ~1,000 requests per month
- Standard quality (excellent for web)

**For Your Needs**:
- ✅ 33 images = ~2-3 days of generation
- ✅ Quality is MORE than sufficient for business websites
- ✅ No credit card required
- ✅ Can generate anytime, no rush

**Pros**:
- Free forever (with reasonable daily limits)
- High quality output
- Fast generation (15-30 seconds)
- Easy to use
- No commitment

**Cons**:
- Daily rate limits (need to space out generation)
- Can't batch generate 33 images in one sitting
- Might need 2-3 days to complete all images

**Verdict**: ✅ **SUFFICIENT for your current needs**

---

### **Option 2: Gemini Pro (Paid)**

**Cost**: $20/month (Google One AI Premium)

**Limits**:
- Significantly higher rate limits
- Priority access
- Gemini 2.0 Pro model
- ~2M tokens context

**For Your Needs**:
- ✅ Could generate all 33 images in one sitting
- ✅ Slightly better quality (marginal difference)
- ❌ Overkill for current needs

**Cost per Image**: ~$0.60 (if only using for this project)

**Verdict**: ❌ **Not worth it for 33 images**

---

### **Option 3: Midjourney Subscription**

**Plans**:
- **Basic**: $10/month - 3.3 hours GPU time (~200 images)
- **Standard**: $30/month - 15 hours GPU time (~900 images)
- **Pro**: $60/month - 30 hours GPU time

**For Your Needs**:
- 33 images = ~0.5-1 hour GPU time
- Basic plan ($10) would cover it with plenty left

**Pros**:
- Best-in-class quality for artistic images
- Great for hero backgrounds
- Commercial use license included
- Batch generation with /imagine command

**Cons**:
- Discord interface (learning curve)
- Overkill for business/tech images
- Same or lower quality than Gemini for photorealistic business photos

**Cost per Image**: ~$0.05-0.15

**Verdict**: ⚠️ **Good but unnecessary** - Gemini free is comparable for business photos

---

### **Option 4: ChatGPT Plus (DALL-E 3)**

**Cost**: $20/month

**Limits**:
- 40 images per 3 hours (slow rate limit!)
- 33 images would take ~3-4 hours of generation time
- Need to space requests

**Pros**:
- Integrated with ChatGPT
- Good understanding of prompts

**Cons**:
- SLOW rate limits (biggest drawback)
- Images tend to be more artistic than photorealistic
- Struggles with text in images
- Expensive for image-only use

**Cost per Image**: ~$0.60

**Verdict**: ❌ **Not recommended** - Slow limits, expensive, Gemini is better for business

---

### **Option 5: Leonardo.ai**

**Cost**: 
- **Free**: 150 tokens/day (~15-20 images)
- **Apprentice**: $10/month - 8,500 tokens
- **Artisan**: $24/month - 25,000 tokens

**For Your Needs**:
- Free tier: Generate all 33 images in 2-3 days
- $10 tier: Generate everything in one day

**Pros**:
- Excellent for photorealistic images
- Great for business/professional photos
- Multiple models (including their own Phoenix)
- Can train custom models (for consistent agent headshots!)

**Cons**:
- Free tier has daily limits
- UI can be overwhelming

**Cost per Image**: ~$0.01-0.03

**Verdict**: ✅ **BEST VALUE if paying** - Excellent for business photos, cheap, high quality

---

### **Option 6: Stock Photo Subscriptions**

**Unsplash**: Free (but limited business/Paraguay-specific content)
**Shutterstock**: $29/month for 10 images
**Adobe Stock**: $29.99/month for 10 images
**Getty Images**: Expensive ($50-500 per image!)

**For Your Needs**:
- Need specific Paraguay/Uruguay real estate + business scenes
- Stock photos won't have Paraguay-specific content
- Can't find "Nexa Paraguay agent headshots"
- Limited authentic Paraguay real estate imagery

**Verdict**: ❌ **Not suitable** - Won't have your specific location/content needs
