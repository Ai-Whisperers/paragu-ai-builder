## ✨ Tips for Best Results

### With Google Gemini:
1. Use **Gemini 2.0 Flash** or **Gemini 2.0 Pro** for best quality
2. Add at end: `"Generate high quality, photorealistic image. Professional photography style."`
3. Download at highest resolution available
4. Use PNG format for best quality

### With Midjourney:
1. Add parameters: `--ar 16:9` for hero, `--ar 4:3` for cards
2. Add: `--style raw --s 250` for photorealistic results
3. Use: `--v 6` or `--v 6.1` for latest version

### With DALL-E 3:
1. Specify: "Professional photograph, 8K quality"
2. Use "Vivid" style for marketing images
3. Download at full resolution
